FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need an appointment, or can I walk in? +

Walk-ins are welcome, but you'll wait less if you book. WhatsApp us or use the online form — it takes under a minute.

How much does scaling / extraction / whitening cost? +

It depends on the case — gum condition for scaling, whether the tooth is impacted for extraction, single-visit vs. take-home for whitening. WhatsApp us your situation and we'll give an upfront estimate.

Which languages do the dentists speak? +

English, Mandarin, and Bahasa Malaysia. Some Cantonese on request.

Do you take insurance or company panels? +

We can issue receipts for reimbursement; for direct panel billing, message us with your insurer's name and we'll confirm.

Is there parking? +

Yes — street parking along Jalan SS26/9 and the Mayang Plaza shoplots. Plenty of space outside peak hours.

Do you see children? +

Yes — gentle paediatric care including first visits, fluoride treatments, and fissure sealants. Dr. April is especially good with younger patients.

Pain doesn't keep office hours. Neither do we.
